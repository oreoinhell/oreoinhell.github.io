# Story 2 The Tragedy of Phaëthon

## 涉及语法

> Second Declension Neuters; Adjectives;
Present Indicative of Sum;
Predicate Nouns & Adjectives; Substantive Adjectives (第四课)

### 第二变格：中性部分 (Dēclīnātiō Secunda: Nōmina Neutra)

这些中性名词通常以*-um*结尾。范例名词: dōnum, -ī（礼物）

|格 (Cāsūs)|单数 (Singulāris)|复数 (Plūrālis)|
|:---|:---|:---|
|**主格**|dōnum|dōna|
|**属格**|dōnī|dōnōrum|
|**与格**|dōnō|dōnīs|
|**宾格**|dōnum|dōna|
|**夺格**|dōnō|dōnīs|
|**呼格**|dōnum|dōna|

### 系动词sum的现在时不定式与现在时直陈式 (Infīnītīvus et Indicātīvus Praesēns)

现在时不定式: esse

现在时直陈式

||单数 (Singulāris)|复数 (Plūrālis)|
|:---|:---|:---|
|**第一人称 (1st Pers.)**|sum|sumus|
|**第二人称 (2nd Pers.)**|es|estis|
|**第三人称 (3rd Pers.)**|est|sunt|

### 谓语性名词与谓语性形容词 (Praedicātum Nōmināle)

Subject (Nom.) + Sum + Predicate (Nom.)

数与格与主语保持一致，（形容词的性也要和主语保持一致）

例句： Sumus bonī amīcī. (We are good friends.)

### 形容词作名词 (Adiectīvum Substantīvum)

非中性的形容词作名词表示某类人，中性的形容词作名词表示某类事物。

## 故事介绍

This myth illustrates the foolishness of attempting a task before one is prepared
for it.

## 正文

{{< book >}}

Phaëthon est fīlius Phoebī. Amīcus Phaëthontis dē fāmā dīvīnae orīginis dubitat: "Nōn es fīlius deī. Nōn habēs dōna dōrum. Nōn vēra est tua fābula." Magna īra Phaëthontem movet: "Fīlius deī sum! Phoebe, dā signum!" vocat Phaëthon. Phoebus puerum auscultat et sina morā volat dē caelō. "Ō, mī fīlī, quid dēsīderās?" Phoebus rogat. "Pecūniam? Sapientiam? Vītam sine cūrīs?" Respondet Phaëthon, "Habēnās habēre et currum sōlis agere dēsīderō." Ō stulte puer! Malum est tuum cōnsilium. Nōn dēbēs officia deōrum dēsīderāre. Pheobus fīlium monet, sed puer magna perīcula nōn videt. Equī valent; nōn valet Phaëthon. Currus sine vērō magistrō errat in caelō. Quid vidēmus? Dē caelō cadit Phaëthon. Ō mala fortūna!

<split>

Phaëthon is the son of Phoebus. A friend of Phaëthon doubts the rumor of divine origin: "You are not the son of a god. You do not have the gifts of gods. Your story is not true." Great ire moves Phaëthon : "I am son of god! Phoebus, give me proof!" Phaëthon says. Phoebus listens to the boy and instantly flys from the sky. "Oh, my son, what do you want?" Phoebus asks. "Money? Wisdom? Life without cares?" Phaëthon responds, "I want to hold the reins and to drive chariot of the sun." Oh foolish boy! Your judgement is bad. You should not want the duties of the gods. Pheobus warns his son, but the boy does not see the great dangers. The horses are strong; Phaëthon is not strong. The chariot without a real master runs wild in the sky. What do we see? Phaëthon falls fron the sky. Oh, such a poor fate!

## 个人评述

法厄同的故事是《变形记》中的经典内容。我还记得随后作者描绘了古罗马人认知中的诸多名山大川遭受太阳马车炙烤。这也是泰坦神最后的余晖。在此之后，日月神便由阿波罗与阿尔忒弥斯接任。

书中说这个故事的警示是在做好准备前就尝试是愚蠢的；但法厄同真的是因为“没有做好准备”而失败的吗？原文便说了“Nōn dēbēs officia deōrum dēsīderāre”，这意味着法厄同是永远不可能以半神之躯驾驶神祇的太阳马车的。

奥维德原文中还写了福波斯给儿子的最后忠告：“Mediō tūtissimus ībīs.”但对于没有力量、在太阳马车上甚至无法看清路途的法厄同而言，这又有什么意义呢？

作为一个典型的古罗马悲剧，法厄同是在试图跨越人与神的界限时失败了，这是非常悲壮的事。Hīc situs est Phaëthōn, currūs aurīga paternī; quem sī nōn tenuit, magnīs tamen excidit ausīs.
